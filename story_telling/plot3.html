I am a coffe bean. I grew up in a coffee plantation in Indonesia, with all the other coffee beans, bathed in the sun and fed by the rich soil.

But one day, a dark-skinned man plucked me and my friends, took us away from our home, and a moment later, I was placed in a curved container.

The head of a giant cat popped up and licked me together with other coffee beans into its mouth. It was hot, humid and rancid, and I had to watch my companions dissolve, one by one, into unknown liquids and mashes and . I felt terrible.

I do not know how long after, I finally squeezed out from a gap, and got to take a breathe of the fresh air again. I was polished, washed, packaged and put on a plane for a new journey with many of my new companions who were as clean as I was.

Then we all came to the last stop of life, Germany. After fine grinding and processing, we became a cup of mellow coffee. My body has disintegrated, but I am really proud of my dedication.
